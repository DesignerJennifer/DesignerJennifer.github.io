About Me

Jennifer Larson is a graphic designer and asipring web developer from Kansas City. After reciving her Bachelor's from The University of Centeral Missouri, Jennifer began her career as a print designer. With a desire to learn more about web and app development, she enrolled in the fall session of the KU Coding Bookcamp.

When not seeking personal and professional impromvents, Jennifer volunteers with Camp Quest Kansas City. Camp Quest Kansas City is a secular week-long sleep away camp for children 8-17, focusing on science and philisophical education. As a founding member, Jennifer has served on the Board and as held varous other leadership roles, since it's founding in 2012. Jennifer is serving her 4th year as Camp Director for the 2020 camp session.

In her spare time Jennifer loves spening time with friends, making crafts or attending costume parties. She was introduced to morel hunting that she does every spring with her Fiance, enjoys playing video games and spoiling her adorable elderly cat, Heidi.
